Color Theory

Color theory is the science and art of using color. It's a set of principles that help people understand how humans perceive color, how colors mix, and how colors contrast with each other. Color theory also involves the messages colors communicate and how to replicate colors.


Basic Three Components

The basic three components of color are hue, value, and saturation. Understanding these components is critical for creating beautiful color harmonies.


Major Seven Color Schemes

The seven major color schemes are monochromatic, analogous, complementary, split complementary, triadic, square, and rectangle (or tetradic).
